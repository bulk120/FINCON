Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2023

FINCON Financial Services values your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website or services.

By accessing our website or submitting your information, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when filling out enquiry forms or contacting us through our website.

The information collected may include, but is not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address
  • Location details
  • Financial requirements and loan-related information

This information is collected solely to understand your requirements and provide appropriate financial assistance.

How We Use Your Information

The personal information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries and service requests
  • To provide financial consultation and assistance
  • To communicate via phone calls, SMS, emails, and WhatsApp
  • To improve our services and customer experience

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Information Sharing & Disclosure

Your information may be shared with banks, NBFCs, or financial institutions only when necessary to process your financial application or enquiry.

Such sharing is strictly limited to service fulfillment and conducted in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

We may also disclose information when required by law or regulatory authorities.

Data Security

FINCON Financial Services takes reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or loss.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies & Website Usage

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website performance.

Cookies do not collect personally identifiable information and are used only to improve website functionality.

You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.

User Consent

By submitting your personal information through our website, you consent to its collection, use, and disclosure as described in this Privacy Policy.

You also authorize FINCON Financial Services to contact you via phone, SMS, email, or WhatsApp regarding your enquiry or related services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

FINCON Financial Services reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice.

Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. We encourage users to review this policy periodically.
